Post Brexit impact to Commercial Real Estate Sector - the experts insights

Date: Wednesday 31st January 2018 Venue: The RDS - Dublin

The Commercial real estate sector has witnessed a dramatic turnaround in the last 3 years. Foreign Inward investment continues at pace, unemployment is now approaching an historic low and the opportunity presented by BREXIT suggests the sector will continue to grow strongly in the next two years.

To realise this potential we are faced with many challenges central to which is the continuing poor pace of change within our Banking sector coupled with the never before seen housing crisis. The 2018 summit will bring together business leaders, professional advisors and all key stakeholders in the Irish commercial real estate sector to robustly debate and challenge the policy makers who influence the shape of tomorrows property sector. The summit will be an opportunity to question industry leaders on how best to maximise the potential of Irelands real estate sector

Why attend this conference?

  • Get an insight into the attitude of the global real estate sector to Ireland Inc
  • Hear from industry leaders on how they intend to challenge the policy makers whose decisions will influence the strength of Irelands Commercial real estate Sector
  • Debate the necessary changes and innovations needed to allow the market to evolve;
  • Network with other stakeholders in the real estate industry and become more informed on the likely future direction of the industry post brexit
